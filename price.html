Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services

What's the difference between the packages?

Each package offers different levels of service intensity and features. The Basic Package is ideal for small businesses just starting with digital marketing, the Intermediate Package offers more comprehensive coverage across platforms, while the Advanced Package provides the highest level of customization and support for businesses with aggressive growth goals.

Are there any hidden fees or long-term contracts?

No hidden fees at all. The price you see is what you pay, and all included services are clearly outlined. Our standard contracts are month-to-month, though we offer discounts for 6-month and 12-month commitments.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my package?

Yes, you can upgrade your package at any time. Downgrades can be processed at the end of your current billing cycle. Our team will work with you to ensure a smooth transition between packages.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee for all our packages. If you're not satisfied with our services, you can request a refund within the first 14 days of your subscription. For monthly subscriptions, we can provide a prorated refund for unused services if cancelled mid-month. Please contact our support team for more details on our refund process.

How soon will I see results?

While some results can be seen immediately (especially with paid advertising), significant organic growth typically takes 3-6 months. We provide monthly reports to track progress and will adjust strategies as needed to optimize results.

Do I need to provide my own ad budget?

Yes, the ad budget is separate from our management fees. We will provide recommendations for optimal budget allocation based on your goals and industry, with complete transparency on how your advertising dollars are being spent.
